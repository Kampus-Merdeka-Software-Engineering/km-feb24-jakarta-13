About Us

Superstore13-jkt Analytics is a specialised analytics platform dedicated to providing comprehensive information on the sales profitability of superstores. We provide deep insights into the financial performance of superstores, with a clear emphasis on the profitability aspect. With a sharp focus on profitability information, we provide superstores with a very useful tool to manage and improve their overall financial performance.

Our team consists of passionate and talented individuals, with expertise in areas such as software engineering, deployment team, pitch deck team, and quality assurance. Together, we are committed to delivering innovative and high-quality solutions to our clients. With our experience and dedication, we believe that we can be a reliable partner in supporting the achievement of your superstore's financial goals.

We believe that collaboration and diversity are key to bringing the most effective and relevant solutions to our clients. Therefore, we are always open to discuss and collaborate with superstores who want to optimise their financial performance. Thank you for trusting Superstore13-jkt Analytics as your analytics partner.
